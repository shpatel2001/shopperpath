ShopperPath is an AI‑powered tool that helps Instacart shoppers work faster, smarter, and with more confidence.  
Upload your shopping list and ShopperPath instantly analyzes items, predicts stock risk, suggests substitutions, and generates an optimized route through the store.

---

##  Why ShopperPath Matters

Instacart shoppers face constant challenges:

- Unclear or incomplete item descriptions  
- Frequent out‑of‑stock items  
- Inefficient store navigation  
- Time pressure to complete batches quickly  
- Customer frustration from poor substitutions  

ShopperPath focuses on three core needs:

- **Accuracy:** Understand exactly what the customer wants  
- **Speed:** Reduce time spent searching for items  
- **Confidence:** Make better substitution decisions backed by data  

This aligns with Instacart’s mission: empowering shoppers with tools that improve efficiency and customer satisfaction.

## Features

- Upload a CSV of items from a batch  
- AI‑powered item interpretation (brand, size, type, dietary tags)  
- Smart substitution suggestions  
- Stock‑risk prediction with shopper actions  
- Route optimization for faster in‑store navigation  
- Clean, simple, mobile‑friendly interface
